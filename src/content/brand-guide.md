# Brand Guide

## Brand Overview
Rabbia Ashraf is a Clinical Child Psychologist based in Lahore, Pakistan, offering local and international consultation, autism/ADHD support, and behavioral interventions.

**Primary Tagline**
*Understanding Behavior. Supporting Development. Guiding Families.*

**Secondary Taglines**
1. Helping Parents Understand the Child Behind the Behavior
2. Evidence-Based Guidance for Children, Parents, and Schools
3. From Behavior Concerns to Clear Support Plans
4. Child Psychology Support for Growing Minds and Families
5. Parent Guidance, Child Development, and Behavior Support

## Brand Meaning
The logo represents an approach where behavior is understood through the lens of child development, care, family support, and clinical evidence.
- **Monogram**: The structured yet soft 'RA' monogram represents professional clinical authority with approachability.
- **Development Dot**: The green circle represents the individual child at the center of the practice, indicating growth and progress.
- **Care Arc/Open Book**: Represents the supportive, educational role of the clinician enveloping the family in an evidence-based framework.
- **Growth Leaf/Curve**: The gentle lavender background element reflects development, nurturing, and adaptation.

## Typography
- **Headings**: Elegant modern serif or refined sans-serif (e.g., Georgia/Merriweather or Inter/Roboto). For the logo wordmark, a classic, highly readable serif font was used to denote clinical authority and professionalism.
- **Body**: Clean accessible sans-serif (e.g., Arial, Inter, Helvetica) for maximum readability for parents and clients.

## Logo Usage Rules

### Minimum Size
- **Primary / Stacked Logo**: Minimum width of 150px for digital, 1.5 inches for print.
- **Icon / Favicon**: Recognizable down to 16x16px.

### Clear Space
Maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "R" in the wordmark to ensure it stands out clearly against other content.

### Light/Dark Usage
- Use `/brand/logo-primary.svg` or `/brand/logo-stacked.svg` on light backgrounds (Warm Cream, White).
- Use `/brand/logo-dark.svg` on dark backgrounds (Deep Navy).
- The text and monogram dynamically flip from Navy to White depending on the background for contrast.

### Icon-Only Usage
The icon (`/brand/logo-icon.svg`) can be used independently for avatars, favicons, or subtle background patterns where the full brand name is already established in context.

### Incorrect Usage
- Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo.
- Do not change the colors outside of the approved brand palette.
- Do not place the dark navy logo on a dark background or the light/white logo on a light background.
- Do not combine the logo with unapproved accreditations, fake badges, or misleading medical symbols.
