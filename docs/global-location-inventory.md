## Priority Tiers Breakdown

### Tier 1 (50 Cities)
Selected based on high overseas Pakistani connection, international school relevance, and English/Arabic/Urdu service accessibility. Includes cities like Lahore, Dubai, London, and New York.

### Tier 2 (150 Cities)
Secondary markets covering broader regions. Features high expatriate presence and strong international school integration. Includes cities like Phoenix, Bristol, Montreal, and Al Ain.

### Tier 3 (300 Cities)
Long-term expansion markets primarily across Europe and other continents. Represents large cities requiring careful localization, translation, and localized legal research before publication.
